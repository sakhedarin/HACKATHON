ABOUT


This website was created to improve residents life griefing for support due to the pandemic attack. It acts as a tool in informing residents about where to get assistance and help. This focuses South African citizens based in Cape Town.
It takes anything that will be helpful starting from an infant baby to old people
BBU is wall about bringing the people together and helping those in need.


INSPIRATION


UBUNTU is a zulu proverb that basically means "I am a person through other people."
One of the saying in our country is "Ubuntu the essence of being human. It speaks about the fact that you can't exist as a human being in isolation."
These are the words of Archbishop Desmond Tutu.
